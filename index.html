Background.

One of my favorite games of all time is Dig Dug. When I was a young child, my mother would give me a handful of quarters to pacify me while she did the grocery shopping at the local Kroger - Savon. I still have fond memories of that beautiful arcade machine located next to the carpet shampoo machines for rent kiosk. I have no experience with programming, but I'm eager - that must account for something; right?

Obstacles

I believe Namco has a pretty significant IP patent on this game, so a big challenge will be creating something that gives the "feel" of the original game without infringing on Namco's original design.

Programming Languages to Consider

I'm intrigued by the possibility of using the Unreal Engine, Swift, Android, etc. because they're all open source. I really want to create a mobile application/game, since I believe this is one of the fastest growing segments of mobile apps./gaming industry.
